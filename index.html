Preparation checklist

  1. Register and review your profile information.
  2. Create a semester plan with destination and travel period.
  3. Add important deadlines and course approval tasks.
  4. Upload passport, insurance and agreement documents.
  5. Check administrator notices before each deadline.
Administrator notice
