Your exchange semester, organized

Plan deadlines, documents and tasks in one place

Erasmus Semester Planner is a web app concept for VDU students who want a clear and simple way to prepare for their exchange semester. Instead of switching between emails, notes, university pages and spreadsheets, students can use one dashboard to track what has to be done before departure.

The planner helps students prepare documents, follow important deadlines, manage personal tasks, and keep all exchange information in a structured timeline. It is designed for fast everyday use on both desktop and mobile devices.

Preparation checklist

  1. Create your semester plan and add your destination details.
  2. Check the application deadline and prepare required forms.
  3. Upload key documents such as Learning Agreement and insurance confirmation.
  4. Add tasks for accommodation, tickets, banking and travel planning.
  5. Review reminders regularly so no important step is missed before departure.

Documents

Store important exchange files in one place and keep track of what has already been prepared.

Helpful information

Students often lose time because exchange planning is spread across many sources. This planner brings the most important information into a simple and guided workflow.

Keep your semester plan updated every week and review pending tasks before each deadline.
