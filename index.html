LD3 MVP GUI based on LD2

Plan your Erasmus semester in one place

This frontend prototype is based on the LD2 project “Erasmus Semester Planner for VDU Students”, where the main goal is to centralize tasks, documents, deadlines and guidance for exchange students. The page demonstrates the MVP screens and interactions a student would use before leaving for Erasmus.

According to the LD2 specification, the MVP should support registration, profile editing, semester plan creation, task management, document storage, a predefined checklist, dashboard reminders, and administrator announcements. Preparation checklist

  1. Register in the system and complete profile information.
  2. Create an Erasmus semester plan with destination and dates.
  3. Add required tasks with deadlines and statuses.
  4. Upload essential documents such as Learning Agreement and insurance.
  5. Check announcements and reminders from the administrator.
