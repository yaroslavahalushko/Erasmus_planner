What the planner helps you do

The MVP focuses on the most important steps before an Erasmus semester starts.

👤

Create your student profile

Store your name, email, destination country, semester and personal preparation notes in one place.

✅

Track deadlines and tasks

Keep a clear list of application tasks, course approvals, insurance steps and travel preparation items.

📄

Organize important documents

See uploaded file names, prepare your Learning Agreement and keep the required document list visible.

How the workflow looks

1

Sign in

Open the planner and switch between login and registration.

2

Set profile

Choose your destination and enter the semester details.

3

Manage tasks

Add, complete and remove preparation tasks dynamically.

4

Check documents

Preview uploaded files and monitor missing requirements.

Why this is useful

  • One place for semester information instead of scattered notes.
  • Better overview of deadlines and incomplete steps.
  • Cleaner preparation before departure and arrival abroad.
  • Useful for both desktop and mobile browsing.
