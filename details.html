Student guide

A simple overview of what students usually need to prepare before the Erasmus semester begins.

Preparation timeline

  • Step 1

    Choose destination and semester

    Confirm your mobility period, destination country and possible host university options.

  • Step 2

    Prepare application

    Collect academic information, personal details and all required documents before submission.

  • Step 3

    Complete Learning Agreement

    Choose tentative courses, confirm recognition rules and request signatures on time.

  • Step 4

    Handle travel and insurance

    Check health insurance, passport validity, accommodation and transport planning.

Typical student questions

  • Which documents are usually required?
    Application form, Learning Agreement, insurance confirmation and identity documents.
  • What should be planned first?
    Destination, mobility dates and major academic deadlines.
  • Why is a task list useful?
    It helps students track what is done and what still requires action.

Recommended preparation checklist

  • Review exchange application dates
  • Prepare Learning Agreement draft
  • Save scans of important documents
  • Explore accommodation options
  • Monitor host university announcements
