Student guide

This page gives students a quick overview of how to use the Erasmus Semester Planner during the preparation period.

Start by creating your semester plan, then add tasks for documents, accommodation, flights, insurance and meetings with your coordinator. Use the dashboard to check what is still pending and review reminders before every important deadline.

  • Track all preparation steps in one place
  • Keep documents and deadlines visible
  • Use the planner on desktop and mobile devices

The planner is designed as a simple first version of a real exchange support tool: focused on clarity, speed and practical student needs.
